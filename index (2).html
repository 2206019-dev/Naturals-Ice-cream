✦ Our Story

Hinjawadi's Most
Coveted Address

Nestled on the first floor of White Square, Maheshwari Link Road, Cherie Kitchen & Bar is Hinjawadi's premier destination for those who seek the finest in multicuisine dining, artisanal cocktails, and an atmosphere that is equal parts intimate and electric.

From the moment you step in, Cherie envelops you in cozy elegance — dramatic lighting, eye-catching décor, and an outdoor seating enclave perfect for Pune's golden evenings. Our menu spans the globe: Modern Indian, Continental, Italian, Pan-Asian, and Chinese cuisines, all crafted with obsessive attention to quality.
