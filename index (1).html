✦ Our Story

A Canvas for Every Sense

Born in the heart of Pune, Artizen is more than a café — it's a living, breathing creative space where the aroma of freshly brewed coffee mingles with the scent of oil paints. We believe every meal should be an experience, and every brush stroke should be savoured.

Whether you're here to devour a wood-fired pizza, attend a watercolour masterclass, or simply find inspiration over a pour-over, Artizen is your canvas.
